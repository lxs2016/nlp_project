## StoryWeaver user study questionnaire (Likert 1–5)

Please play for **15–20 minutes** (at least one full trajectory), then answer:

### Likert questions (1 = strongly disagree, 5 = strongly agree)
- Q1. I felt **immersed** in the story world.
- Q2. The story was **coherent** (no obvious contradictions).
- Q3. My actions felt **controllable** (choices led to understandable consequences).
- Q4. The system response felt **fast enough** for interactive play.
- Q5. Overall, I am **satisfied** with the experience.

### Open questions
- O1. What did you like the most?
- O2. What confused you the most / what should be improved?
